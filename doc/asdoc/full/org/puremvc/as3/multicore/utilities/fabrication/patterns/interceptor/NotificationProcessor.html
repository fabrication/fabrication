Classpublic class NotificationProcessor
InheritanceNotificationProcessor flash.events.EventDispatcher
ImplementsIDisposable

The NotificationProcessor aggregates interceptors for a notification and sends proceeds, abort or skip notification when interceptors added to it perform the corresponding interception.
